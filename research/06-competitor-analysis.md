# PriceLabs Competitor Analysis

## Market Landscape

The STR dynamic pricing market has 4 major players:
1. **PriceLabs** - Deepest customization, most integrations
2. **Beyond Pricing** - Simplest setup, percentage-based
3. **Wheelhouse** - Free tier, visual comp sets
4. **DPGO** - Cost-per-night model, strong event detection

---

## PriceLabs vs Beyond Pricing

| Dimension | PriceLabs | Beyond Pricing |
|-----------|-----------|----------------|
| **Pricing Model** | $19.99/listing/month or 1% | 1-2% of booking revenue |
| **Setup Time** | 2-3 hours | 20 minutes |
| **Customization Depth** | ~47 adjustable settings | ~5 adjustable settings |
| **PMS Integrations** | 160+ | 20+ |
| **Best Data Markets** | Secondary/tertiary US + international | Top-tier US metros |
| **Min Stay Control** | Best-in-class, day-of-week level | Basic |
| **Orphan Day Mgmt** | Best-in-class | Basic |
| **Extra Features** | None | Relay (cross-platform sync), Health Score |
| **Free Trial** | 30 days, no CC | $50 credit |
| **Best For** | 3+ units, data-savvy, international | 1-3 units, simplicity, major metros |

**Cost comparison at scale:**
- 20-unit portfolio grossing $80K/month:
  - PriceLabs: ~$320/month
  - Beyond Pricing: ~$800/month

**Best markets for each:**
- PriceLabs: Gatlinburg, Branson, Gulf Shores, Destin, Sedona + international
- Beyond: NYC, LA, Miami, Nashville, Austin, San Diego

## PriceLabs vs Wheelhouse

| Dimension | PriceLabs | Wheelhouse |
|-----------|-----------|------------|
| **Pricing** | $19.99/listing/mo or 1% | Free tier; paid $19.99/listing/mo or 1% |
| **Setup Time** | 2-3 hours | 45 minutes |
| **Key Strength** | Depth of customization | Visual comp set builder, RevPAR focus |
| **Strategy Options** | Algorithm-driven + customization | Rules-based, data-driven, OR hybrid |
| **PMS Integrations** | 160+ | 15+ |
| **International** | Strong (150+ countries) | Thinner |
| **Support Response** | Hours | 24-48 hours |
| **Best For** | Power users, large portfolios | Budget-conscious first-timers |

**Wheelhouse unique advantage:** Only tool offering choice between rules-based, data-driven, or hybrid pricing strategies. Genuine free tier (not stripped-down).

## PriceLabs vs DPGO

| Dimension | PriceLabs | DPGO |
|-----------|-----------|------|
| **Pricing** | $19.99/listing/mo or 1% | $1/booked night or 0.5% |
| **Data Inputs** | Extensive market data | 200+ local market parameters |
| **Forecast Window** | 180 days | 52 weeks (longer) |
| **Event Detection** | 4-way system | Innovative, more sources |
| **PMS Integrations** | 160+ | 10+ |
| **Coverage** | 150+ countries | US/Canada focus |
| **Algorithm Tendency** | Balanced revenue/occupancy | Prioritizes occupancy |
| **Best For** | Customization, large portfolios | Cost minimizers, event markets |

**Key DPGO concern:** In a 4-month controlled trial on parallel identical listings, ADR fell 12% compared to PriceLabs, though occupancy improved. The algorithm is more aggressive on discounting.

## Competitive Moats

### PriceLabs Strengths (vs all competitors)
1. **152+ PMS integrations** (nearest competitor: ~20)
2. **47+ customization settings** (nearest: ~10)
3. **Flat monthly fee** saves money at scale
4. **Day-of-week minimum stay** controls (unique)
5. **Orphan day management** (best-in-class)
6. **International coverage** (150+ countries)
7. **Free Portfolio Analytics**
8. **4-way event detection** system

### PriceLabs Weaknesses (vs competitors)
1. **Steeper learning curve** (2-3 hours setup vs 20 min)
2. **No free tier** (Wheelhouse has one)
3. **No mobile app**
4. **UI described as "clunky" and "dated"**
5. **Weaker in top-tier US metros** (Beyond has better data)
6. **No cross-platform sync tool** (Beyond has Relay)
7. **Customer support inconsistency**

## When to Choose Each

| Scenario | Recommended Tool |
|----------|-----------------|
| 1-2 properties, want simplicity | Beyond Pricing |
| Want to test dynamic pricing for free | Wheelhouse |
| 3+ properties, want maximum control | **PriceLabs** |
| Operating internationally | **PriceLabs** |
| Large portfolio (20+ units) | **PriceLabs** (cost advantage) |
| Event-heavy market, cost-sensitive | DPGO |
| Top-tier US metro (NYC, LA, Miami) | Beyond Pricing |
| Secondary US market (Gatlinburg, Destin) | **PriceLabs** |
| Hotels and aparthotels | **PriceLabs** |
