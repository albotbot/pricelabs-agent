## Product Suite (5 Products)

### 1. Dynamic Pricing (Core Product)
- Automated rate adjustments using real-time market data
- Powered by the **Hyper Local Pulse (HLP)** algorithm
- Factors: demand, seasonality, local events, competitor pricing, booking pace
- 47+ adjustable customization settings
- Pushes rates for 540 days (extendable to 720)
- Syncs 1-2x daily (default: 6pm-6am Chicago time)

**Pricing:** $19.99/listing/month OR 1% of booking revenue
- Volume discounts available at scale

### 2. Market Dashboard
- Standalone market intelligence tool (no Dynamic Pricing required)
- Data from Airbnb, Vrbo, and KeyData
- Up to 20 Custom Comp Sets per dashboard (expandable to 30)
- 40+ filters for tailoring competitive data
- Metrics: Average Revenue, RevPAR, ADR, Occupancy, Booking Window, LOS trends

**Pricing:**
| Tier | Listings | Price |
|------|----------|-------|
| Basic | 1,000 listings | $9.99/mo |
| Standard | 5,000 listings | $19.99/mo |
| Premium | 10,000 listings | $39.99/mo |

### 3. Portfolio Analytics (FREE)
- Real-time performance reporting for all connected listings
- No Dynamic Pricing subscription required
- KPIs: Revenue, ADR, Occupancy, RevPAR, Avg LOS, Avg Lead Time, Reservation Count
- Pacing reports comparing future vs. past performance
- Interactive scatter chart for listing-level comparison
- Custom report builder with data export

### 4. Revenue Estimator Pro
- Property revenue estimation for investment evaluation
- Customizable market comparables
- Scenario testing for pricing strategies

**Pricing:**
| Tier | Estimates | Price |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Starter | 2/month | $10/mo |
| Growth | 15/month | $45/mo |
| Pro | 50/month | $125/mo |

### 5. Listing Optimizer
- AI-powered Airbnb listing content optimization
- Quality grades A through D (weighted scoring)
- Factors: title, photos, description, amenities, ratings, reviews, Guest Favorites status
- Competitive analysis against top performers
- Search ranking tracking over time
- **Airbnb only** (current limitation)

**Pricing:** Per listing, tiered structure (monthly subscription or one-time)
